Who We Are

Ajuda is portuguese for “help” and our purpose is to improve employees´ lives through benefits. Ajuda was founded by Ana Luis and Jonathan Burger who both envisioned a new way forward for HR and benefits.

As a company, Ajuda strongly believes in inclusion & diversity. We strive to create a diverse workforce and give opportunities to those usually underrepresented.
